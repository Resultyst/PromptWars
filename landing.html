⚠ The Problem

Crowds Don't Warn You.
We Do.

Every major sporting event is a controlled chaos. When systems fail, the results are catastrophic. The Chinnaswamy Stadium incident of 2024, Hillsborough, the Itaewon crush — all shared one common thread: no real-time intelligence.

🌊
Overcrowding Beyond Capacity
Zones fill beyond safe limits with no automated alerting system. Security relies on manual observation — a process fundamentally too slow for crowd dynamics.
Primary risk factor in 78% of stadium incidents
🚪
Gate Bottlenecks
During peak ingress and egress — matches starting, half-time, and post-match — single-gate dependency creates fatal chokepoints that cascade into stampede conditions.
Gates failed in 100% of major stadium disasters
📡
Zero Real-Time Communication
Fans have no live guidance. PA systems are reactive. By the time an announcement reaches fans, the zone is already critical. "Calm messaging" on top of panic rarely works.
Average warning lag: 4–12 minutes
🔮
No Predictive System
Current solutions are entirely reactive. Nobody predicts half-time concession surges, end-of-match exit rushes, or weather-driven indoor crowding 30 minutes in advance.
Prediction window needed: 15–30 min
🚑
Poor Emergency Coordination
Security, medical, and fire teams operate in silos. No shared ops picture, no unified routing for evacuation, no automated task assignment during emergencies.
Response time gap: 6–18 min in major events
🏏
The Chinnaswamy Context
M. Chinnaswamy Stadium, Bengaluru hosts 38,000+ fans for high-profile IPL matches. Its compact layout and single-road access make it one of India's highest-risk venues during peak events.
Capacity: 38,000 | IPL matches: sold-out

⚡ Features

Everything in one
operations center.

🗺
Live Crowd Heatmap
Top-down stadium visualization with real-time density overlay. Green to red gradient shows crowd pressure per zone, updated every second. Hover any zone for instant analytics.
🚨
AI Alert Engine
Automatic severity-graded alerts the moment density exceeds defined thresholds. Critical, Danger, Warning — each with specific recommended actions and routing.
🔀
Smart Fan Routing
When a zone exceeds 75% capacity, FlowSense AI identifies lower-density alternative gates and pushes personalized directions to fans via the FlowSense app.
📱
Fan App Integration
Every spectator carries a real-time guide in their pocket. The FlowSense Fan App shows their zone, recommended route, concession queue times, and emergency directions.
🚨
Emergency Protocol
One-click emergency activation reroutes all 38,000 fans to safe exit paths. Security, medical, and fire teams receive simultaneous task alerts with precise location data.
📊
Live Ops Feed
Integrated notification feed showing every system action — fan rerouting, queue changes, vendor wait times, security deployments — as they happen in real time.
⚙ How It Works

Three layers.
Zero guesswork.

01
Sense
Distributed sensors + computer vision feed real-time crowd density data for every zone, gate, concourse, and concession stand into FlowSense AI.
02
Predict
The AI model forecasts congestion 30 minutes ahead by combining live density, match timeline events, historical patterns, and weather data.
03
Act
Automated alerts, smart fan routing via the app, and security task dispatch happen simultaneously — before crowds reach dangerous densities.
